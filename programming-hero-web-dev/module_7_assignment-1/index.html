Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"


Innovation

Creating groundbreaking solutions through new ideas and technologies to drive progress, improve efficiency, and transform industries in a rapidly evolving world.

Sustainability

Balancing economic growth with environmental responsibility by adopting eco-friendly practices to preserve resources for future generations.

Infrastructure

Building resilient systems and facilities to support economic growth, enhance connectivity, and improve quality of life for communities.

Empowerment

Enabling individuals and communities with knowledge, tools, and opportunities to achieve self-reliance, personal growth, and meaningful societal contributions

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation
Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.
Green Energy
Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.
Modern Infrastructure
Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
"Bangladesh 2.0 represents a bold vision of innovation, sustainability, and empowerment. It embodies a transformative journey, driven by the collective resilience and ingenuity of its people, embracing technology, green energy, and inclusive development to achieve economic growth and social equity. Together, we are building a brighter, sustainable future for all."
Dr. Muhammad Yunus
Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
